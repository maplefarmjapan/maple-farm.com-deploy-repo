Maple Farm

Maple Farm is a private farmhouse rental — a former farmhouse on quiet countryside land, not a working farm or farm-stay. Located just minutes from Asahikawa Airport (AKJ) and Biei's famous attractions, it offers a comfortable blend of convenience and tranquility.

Whether you're here for the summer flower fields, winter illuminations, or year-round cultural experiences, Maple Farm serves as your ideal base for exploring all that this beautiful region has to offer.

Location

Maple Farm is perfectly located just 5 minutes drive from Asahikawa Airport (AKJ), making it an ideal base for exploring the famous Blue Pond, Patchwork Road, and the beautiful flower fields of Biei — with Asahiyama Zoo just 20 minutes by car.

The property offers easy access to all major attractions while providing a peaceful countryside setting.

⬆️

Transport and Access

Flights to Asahikawa Airport:
Tokyo Haneda (HND – JAL/AirDo, daily year-round, approx. 1 hr 50 min), Tokyo Narita (NRT – Jetstar, direct budget option), Nagoya Chubu (NGO – ANA, July–October), and Osaka Itami (ITM – JAL, from August 2026). Seoul Incheon (ICN) and Taipei Taoyuan (TPE) routes have operated as winter-season services in recent years — most overseas visitors connect via Tokyo Haneda (HND) or New Chitose (CTS, Sapporo).

From Asahikawa Airport (AKJ):
Taxi (5 minutes; <¥1,500) or rental cars available at the airport.

By Train:
Chiyogaoka Station (JR Furano Line) is a 20-minute walk from the house.

Getting Around:
Car rental recommended for maximum flexibility accessing remote attractions.

Bicycle Rentals:
COGICOGI electric-assist share bikes (self-serve, app-based): ¥3,000 for 24 hours. Nearest pickup point is Asahikawa Airport (AKJ, 7:30–20:30) — see the port map. Alternatively, the Asahikawa Airport Activity Center (1st-floor arrival lobby) rents electric mountain bikes for ¥4,000/day plus a ¥3,000 refundable deposit, 9:00–16:30.

Key Distances By Car:
Blue Pond (35-40 min), Asahiyama Zoo (20 min), Furano (1h), Shikisai Hill (20 min).

Nearby Hot Springs

Maple Farm doesn't have an on-site onsen, but these hot springs are popular day trips nearby:

Kitoron (15 minutes)

Modern spa with design supervision by Kengo Kuma and panoramic views. Finnish-style saunas, outdoor baths overlooking Higashikawa town. Free massage chairs and family-friendly facilities.

Mori no Yu Hanakagura (20 minutes)

Forest onsen with Daisetsuzan mountain views. Outdoor baths under starry skies, cottages with private hot springs. Near Asahikawa Airport and Zoo. Peaceful retreat in nature.

More →Map →
Shirogane Onsen (35-40 minutes)

Beautiful hot spring village near Blue Pond. Known as "hot springs for beautiful skin" with 100% natural flowing water. Multiple hotels and day-trip bathing options available.

Asahidake Onsen (45 minutes)

Ski Resorts

Kamui Ski Links (40 minutes)

The closest major ski area to Maple Farm, with 25 courses from beginner to advanced. Known for excellent powder snow and a relaxed local atmosphere. Check the official site for season dates, hours, and lift prices.

More →Map →
Asahidake (45 minutes)

One of Japan's longest ski seasons (November to May) on Hokkaido's highest peak. Famous for deep powder, backcountry skiing, and stunning mountain views. Advanced terrain recommended for experienced skiers.

More →Map →
Kurodake (1.5 hours)

Part of Daisetsuzan National Park with spectacular alpine scenery. Offers both groomed runs and off-piste opportunities. Ropeway access from Sounkyo for mountain exploration.

More →Map →
Canmore Ski Village (20 minutes)

A smaller, family-friendly ski area perfect for beginners and intermediate skiers. Affordable lift tickets and relaxed atmosphere. Great for families with children learning to ski.

More →Map →
Furano Ski Resort (1h 15min)

Famous resort that hosted FIS World Cup races. Two ski areas (Kitanomine and Furano zones) with varied terrain. Known for excellent snow quality and beautiful views of the Tokachi mountain range.

Useful Info

Best Seasons:
Summer (July-August) for flower fields and mild weather. Winter (December-February) for skiing, the Blue Pond illuminations and Asahikawa Winter Festival.

Cultural Sites:
Asahikawa City Museum showcasing Ainu culture, Otokoyama Sake Brewery for tastings, and traditional Hokkaido handicraft shops.

Weather:
Summer temperatures 15 to 30°C (59-86°F). Winter temperatures -15 to +10°C (5-50°F) with many days of powder snow and blue skies.

Wood Stove (winter only):
The wood stove can be used in winter for an additional ¥10,000 per stay, after agreeing to the usage guidelines (how to operate it and which fuel may be burned).

Frequently Asked Questions

How far is Maple Farm from Asahikawa Airport (AKJ)?

Just 5 minutes by car or taxi. A taxi from the airport costs under ¥1,500.

Do I need a car to stay at Maple Farm?

A rental car is recommended for maximum flexibility, and cars can be rented at Asahikawa Airport (AKJ). Taxis and electric-assist rental bicycles are also available at the airport, 5 minutes away.

How far is the Blue Pond?

Biei's famous Blue Pond is around 35-40 minutes away by car. In winter (late October to late April) the pond is illuminated at night, from sunset until 21:00 (schedule varies).

Does the house have a kitchen?

Yes — a full kitchen with induction cooktop, built-in oven, and microwave. Several supermarkets are within a short drive for groceries and local Hokkaido produce.

When is the best time to visit?

Summer (July to August) for lavender and flower fields, and winter (December to February) for powder snow, skiing, the Blue Pond illuminations, and the Asahikawa Winter Festival.

Which ski resorts are closest?

Canmore Ski Village is 20 minutes away, Kamui Ski Links 40 minutes, and Asahidake — with one of Japan's longest ski seasons — 45 minutes.

Is there parking at Maple Farm?

Yes — free on-site parking is available for guests.

How far is Asahiyama Zoo from Maple Farm?

Asahiyama Zoo, famous for its winter penguin walks, is about 20 minutes away by car.

Can we use the wood stove (fireplace)?

Yes — in winter, for an additional ¥10,000 per stay. We ask you to agree to the usage guidelines (operation and permitted fuel) beforehand.
